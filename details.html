Why inference stacks are redesigning the dev workflow

Fast-moving teams are rewriting the rules of deployment by treating inference as a product layer, not just runtime plumbing.

Jules Wang
• Published on June 18, 2024

Inference stacks have moved out of the shadows. Product teams are now orchestrating prompt routing, fine-tuning, and evaluation directly inside deployment pipelines. That shift is redefining the developer workflow, making iteration loops feel more like product design than infrastructure maintenance.

What stands out is the separation of concerns: retrieval, guardrails, and personalization now live as composable services. The result is a workflow that supports experiments without rewriting everything for production, unlocking a cleaner handoff between research and product teams.
